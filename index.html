Core Features

Everything a ward manager
actually needs

Purpose-built for clinical environments — not retrofitted from generic HR tools.

AI Roster Generation
Generate a compliant, conflict-free draft roster in seconds. The engine accounts for skill mix, rest-period legislation, individual preferences, and historical patterns.
★ Most used
Real-time Conflict Detection
Double-bookings, under-staffed shifts, and award breaches are flagged the moment they occur — with plain-English resolution suggestions and one-click fixes.
Smart Leave Management
Staff submit leave requests via mobile. Managers approve with full coverage-impact visibility. Replacements are auto-suggested from a qualified available pool.
Fatigue & Rest Tracking
Continuous monitoring against EBA and Award obligations. Accumulated hours, mandatory rest periods, and consecutive shift limits are tracked automatically, per staff member.
Compliance
Instant Staff Notifications
Published rosters, shift swaps, and urgent callouts pushed directly to staff via SMS, email, or app. Confirmation receipts logged automatically for compliance records.
Workforce Analytics
Understand your staffing patterns, overtime spend, leave trends, and skill-mix health across wards and departments. Export board-ready reports in one click.
Process

Live in days, not months

We've cut out the implementation complexity that plagues legacy workforce systems.

1
Connect your data
Import staff profiles, qualifications, and historical rosters from your existing HRIS or via CSV. Most teams complete setup in under 2 hours.
2
Configure your rules
Set your Award obligations, skill-mix requirements, and ward preferences. ShiftMD includes pre-built templates for major Australian and NZ Awards.
3
Generate your roster
Hit generate. Review the AI-drafted roster, make any tweaks, then publish directly to staff. Conflicts are caught before you go live.
4
Run and improve
Analytics surface patterns over time. The AI learns your ward's rhythm and gets more accurate with every published roster.
